## Why

I needed a cheap, modular quiver for my bow.

## What

Printed in PETG for extra ruggedness and heat resistance. The quiver is
side-mounted for ease of access, with a mounting plate compatible with my
bow's existing mount and an arrow hood that protects both the arrowheads
and the archer.
